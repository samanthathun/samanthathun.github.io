Wedding Reception Accomodations

Temple Inn & Suites offers a beautiful wedding reception venue for friends and family of the new couple. Enjoy our charming rustic brick reception hall that brings in a bit of history and elegance to any brides dream reception celebration. Our venue offers beauty by the sea, with sunshine just about everyday. You can count on us to bring one of your happiest days become one to remember always.
