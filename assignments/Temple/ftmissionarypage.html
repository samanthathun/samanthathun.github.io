About Our Suites

We love to help senior missionary couples get set up to begin their time serving in the temple and local areas. We offer the perfect suites that have a private bedroom with a king size bed and onsuite bathroom. A cozy living room space and kitchette with all the essetionals. The space is simple, comfortable and relaxing. All our suites have ADA accessible showers and are located near an elevator or on the first level of the hotel. All suites have their own outdoor space or balcony, giving you the opportunity to enjoy our views of the ocean and city with some fresh coastal air. We look forward to helping you get settled in!
