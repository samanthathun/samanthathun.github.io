Autumn is Here! 
by Kyndra Leam

Enjoying the cool crisp air during this time of year brings back memories of when I was a kid taking walks with my grandmother. We would collect as many different colored leaves that we could and take them home and display them in a big clear glass vase. I was always amazed at the beauty and joy that can come by just being out in nature. As we enter this changing time of year, make sure you come out by the lake for a stroll down the trail, bring a picnic, paint, take photographs, make lasting memories with your families in this beautiful place that we live in.
