Local Farmers Market 
by Rebecca Casey

Idaho has some amazing produce to offer, it's so fresh and delicious. Every Saturday morning farmers from southeast Idaho come together to share their homegrown fruits and vegetables with our community. You'll find a wide variety of fresh apples, cherries, peaches, pears, tomatoes, carrots and of course potatoes just to name a few. The atmosphere is pleasant and happy with families and friends coming together to support our local economy. Along with yummy fresh food, there are other vendors offering their homemade soaps, jewelery, and crafts.
